Pine City Mall

Pine City Mall is the ultimate fashion, entertainment and dining destination in Durban. With over 100+ stores, 20 eateries and a host of exciting entertainment, visitors are spoilt for choice! At Pine City you will find local South African retail, popular international brands such as PeP, KFC, SONY and everything in between. Gateway also features an couple of cinema's Ster Kinekor complex, NUMETRO Theatre. Prepare your senses for a world class experience at one of the largest shopping centres in the Southern Hemisphere.
